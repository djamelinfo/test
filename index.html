Short Bio.

I am a third year PhD student in Computer Science at Inria in Montpellier. I work at the Lirmm lab, in the Zenith team under the supervision of Florent Masseglia, Reza Akbarinia and Themis Palpanas. My research topic is about the Big Data Analytics. The title of my PhD thesis is "Massive distribution for indexing and mining time series". I hold an M.Sc in Computer Science from University of Oran 1, Algeria, in 2014. The title of my M.Sc thesis was "The management of energy consumption in Clouds Computing".

Research Interests.

My research interest includes, but not limit to : Time Series Analysise. Parallel Data Mining. Streaming Data Processing. Information Retrieval.
